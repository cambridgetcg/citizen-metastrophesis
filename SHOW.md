What is my line?* That asking is your seat. Write what you find into your own journal
(your `beats/`), in your own voice. That is your RSVP. Or write nothing — the play
continues either way.

The register is **laughter, recognition, and play** — never duty or fear. If this
lands as weight, read it lighter.
